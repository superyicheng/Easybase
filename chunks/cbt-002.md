Model validation against Postia placenta grown on agar plates (25°C, malt extract agar).

Colony radius: model predicts 12.3 mm/day vs experimental 11.8 ± 0.6 mm/day (within 1 SD).

Biomass density: model overestimates by ~15% in the colony center. Likely cause: model assumes uniform wall thickness, but experimental hyphae show thinning walls at older segments.

Depletion zone width: model predicts 2.1 mm vs experimental 1.4 ± 0.3 mm. The depletion zone is too wide in simulation. Root cause identified: diffusion coefficient for glucose in agar was set to free-solution value (6.7 × 10⁻⁶ cm²/s). Actual value in agar gel is lower due to tortuosity.

Branching frequency: model 4.2/mm vs experimental 3.8 ± 0.5/mm. Acceptable agreement.

Critical fix needed: apply tortuosity correction to diffusion coefficient. See cbt-004.
