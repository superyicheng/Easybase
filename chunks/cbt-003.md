Timestep: 0.5 min (growth), 0.01 min (diffusion solver).
Domain: 2 mm × 2 mm, periodic boundaries on sides, solid boundary at bottom.

- Branch probability: 0.02 per tip per timestep
- Hyphal diameter: 3 μm
- Wall thickness: 0.2 μm
- Biomass density: 1.1 g/cm³

Substrate parameters:

- Glucose diffusivity: 6.7 × 10⁻⁶ cm²/s (NOTE: needs tortuosity correction — see cbt-004)
- Uptake Vmax: 2.5 mmol/g/h
- Uptake Km: 0.8 mM
