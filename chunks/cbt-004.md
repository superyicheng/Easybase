Effective diffusion in agar gel is lower than free solution due to tortuosity.

Correction: D_eff = D_free / τ²

For 1.5% agar gel, τ ≈ 1.28 (measured via fluorescence recovery).
D_free(glucose) = 6.7 × 10⁻⁶ cm²/s
D_eff(glucose) = 6.7 × 10⁻⁶ / 1.28² = 4.09 × 10⁻⁶ cm²/s

This 39% reduction in diffusivity narrows the predicted depletion zone from 2.1 mm to ~1.5 mm, matching experimental observation of 1.4 ± 0.3 mm (see cbt-002).

Tortuosity varies with agar concentration:
- 0.5% agar: τ ≈ 1.05
- 1.0% agar: τ ≈ 1.15
- 1.5% agar: τ ≈ 1.28
- 2.0% agar: τ ≈ 1.42

Update cbt-003 simulation config with D_eff = 4.09 × 10⁻⁶ cm²/s for 1.5% agar runs.
